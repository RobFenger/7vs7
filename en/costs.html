What are the costs?

For the calculation of the prices, the assumption is that a team consists of an average of 12 people. We recommend that teams be composed of 12 players or more. The more players, the better. Take into account cancellations and injuries that may prevent people from participating. Furthermore, it is useful to have substitutes during a game.
