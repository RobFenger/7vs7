How does it work?

Everyone can participate. The most important thing is that there are 7 people on the field every match (women are also allowed to participate). We recommend 10 till 12 people per team. Everyone must come up with a team name with a logo (this can be a picture from the internet). The winning team earns 3 points at the end of the game. Equal is 1 point. Losing is 0 points

Arrange yourself:

  • Own uniforms (all the same color shirt or something in the theme of your team name)
  • Soccer boots, shin guards (optional), shorts
  • Own ball to warm up
  • Water
  • Logo for your team name (image from the internet)

From the organization:

  • Referee
  • Matchball
  • Vests (if teams have the same colors)
  • Competition format
  • Location
  • Canteen where drinks can be obtained
  • Changing rooms
  • It can be that simple! For any questions, please send an email toinfo@7versus7.nl
