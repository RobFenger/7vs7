For who is it?

7vs7 is in fact for everyone over the age of 18. It is intended for people who do not have time to play soccer seriously at a club, but still want to play a game every now and then. Moreover, it is nice for companies that are looking for activities for their staff.

Read more

How does it work?

7vs7 is the soccer competition in Haarlem where we don't play 11 against 11, but 7 against 7 on half a field. It's not as coercive as a real soccer club, but casual, where it's all about having fun playing soccer!

Read more

Rules

The rules are simple. They resemble the rules of 'real' soccer, but suitable for 7 against 7 and aimed at keeping it fun for everyone. The most important rule is therefore: Have fun!
