7 Versus 7 is for everyone who loves the game of football. Young people, old people, women, anyone who has never touched a ball but still likes to kick a ball
"I'm someone who likes football but doesn't have time to train twice a week and play a game at the weekend. Football is an outlet for me and I love playing a football game that is also competitive"
"I have a full-time job and no time for selection football or to be present at a club 2 to 3 times a week. Still, I love being able to play a game. There are few opportunities that offer me to still be able to participate in my favorite sport"
"We are a company that always talks about football during breaks. We didn't see any possibility yet to set up a team together. We do think that setting up a football team can be good for motivation within our company and for the team."
"I want to play football but I can't find a team. Still, I really like the game. At a football club I think it all gets a little too serious. I just want to play a game every week"
"I am someone who likes to meet new people. I have no trouble adapting myself and can function well in a team"
"I have a group of friends with whom we would like to start a team. We don't want to have to train every week, but only play matches."
"I just moved from abroad to the Netherlands. I love football but I don't have many people around me who love it too. I would love to be able to kick a ball here every week and get to know new people"
