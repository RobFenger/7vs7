Game form

  • We play on a half field
  • Each match lasts 2x 25 minutes (+ half change)
  • Slides are not allowed. A sliding is only allowed when the opponent is not near the ball
  • Scoring is allowed from any position
  • When the ball is passed back to the goalkeeper and the goalkeeper handles the ball, an indirect free kick is awarded to the opposing team from the spot where the goalkeeper touched the ball with his hands
  • Penalties are taken from 9 metres
  • Free kicks may be taken without a whistle signal
  • A ball must be thrown in from the neck
  • If you play too hard, you will first receive a warning
  • When you receive a yellow card you get a time out for 3 minutes
  • When you receive a red card you will be suspended for the next 2 matches
  • When you receive two red cards the player will be disqualified for the rest of the competition
  • The most important thing for us is that everyone enjoys it. But of course it is competitive
  • There is no offside in 7 Versus 7
  • At the end of the season, a cup is awarded to the number 1 of each league

Game rules

  • As team you can substitute as many times as you want. This way everyone has time to play
  • The referee is always in charge. The referee's choices must be respected at all times
  • We recommend playing with shin guards. It can always happen that someone accidentally hits you (not required)
  • Every match a man of the match is chosen. This person receives a token with which a free drink can be obtained in the canteen
  • The player most often voted man of the match at the end of the competition will receive a prize at the end of the season
  • A dangerous sliding is immediately penalized with a free kick and a warning. The second time will result in a yellow card
  • Each team must wear the same colors of the uniforms. Each team is of course allowed to come up with its own colours
  • For free kicks, a distance of 6 meters must be taken
  • Only the keeper may slide for the ball if it is harmless
  • The organization may always adjust rules to make the game safer and more fun for the players. Safety and fun are our top priorities
  • If you are 10 minutes late at kick-off, the team that is late automatically loses 3-0
  • The goalkeeper may take the ball in his hands up to 6 meters. Too far is an indirect free kick for the opponent
  • A shoulder push is allowed
